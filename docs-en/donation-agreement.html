AGREEMENT – PUBLIC OFFER
about donation

The Fund for support and promotion of innovations in the field of medical technologies “Step into the Future”, hereinafter referred to as the “Foundation”, represented by the Manager Kseniia Ryzhova, acting on the Basis of the Charter, offers any individual (citizen) or legal entity, individual entrepreneur who responded to this proposal, hereinafter referred to as the “Donor”, enter into an Agreement on voluntary donation (hereinafter referred to as the Agreement) on the following conditions:

1. General provisions about this offer

1.1. This offer is a public offer (hereinafter referred to as the “Offer”) in accordance with Law of Obligations of Serbia (Vedomosti SFRY, No. 29/78, 39/85, 45/89 – decision of the Constitutional Court and 57/89, Vedomosti SFRY, No. 31/93, Vedomosti GKG, No. 1/2003 – Constitutional Charter and the Official Gazette of the RS, No. 18/2020), located at https://stepinfond.com

1.2. The offer comes into force on 1st of September, 2024 and is subject to mandatory publication (hereinafter referred to as Publication) at https://stepinfond.com

1.3. Publication, in accordance with the terms of this Agreement, is carried out in one and/or several of the following ways:

1.3.1. Placing published information on the official website of the Foundation and its partners. 1.3.2.

Placement of published information at the address (location) of the Foundation, located at: Serbia, Niš, ul.Rudnička 14,stan br.3.

1.4. This Offer is unlimited.

1.5. The text of this Offer may be changed/added by the Foundation unilaterally without giving reasons or prior notification to third parties, including Donors, of the decision made. The new version of the Offer comes into force from the date of its Publication, unless otherwise provided in the decision to change / supplement the terms of the Offer.

1.6. The Foundation has the right to cancel the Offer at any time without giving reasons or prior notification to third parties, including Donors, of the decision. In this case, the last day of validity of the Offer is the day of publication on the Site of the notice of cancellation of the Offer, unless otherwise provided in the notice of cancellation of the Offer.

1.7. The invalidity of one or more terms of the Offer does not entail the invalidity of all other terms of the Offer.

1.8. By accepting the terms of this Agreement, the Donor confirms the voluntary and gratuitous nature of the donation.

2. Subject of the Agreement

2.1. By accepting this Offer, the Donor voluntarily and free of charge transfers his own funds to the Foundation (hereinafter referred to as the “Donation”) to achieve its statutory goals, including the implementation of its projects and programs.

2.2. The main goal of the Foundation is to attract investments / donations to participate in international projects for the development of medical technologies with their integration throughout the world; assistance and assistance in financing research aimed at combating cancer and autoimmune diseases, including early diagnosis; development of international cooperation in the medical and scientific research fields to make innovations accessible to medical and scientific specialists, medical institutions, research, health, and rehabilitation centers.

2.2.1. The Foundation annually publishes information about its work, its results, and the purposes of using funds in open sources.

2.3. The funds transferred by the Donor, in accordance with the terms of the Agreement, to the Foundation are a donation in accordance with legislation of Serbia

2.4. A donation made in favor of a project or program cannot be canceled (revoked) by the Donor unilaterally, except in cases expressly provided for by law and this Offer.

3. Conclusion of the Agreement

3.1. Any legally capable individual (citizen) or legal entity, individual entrepreneur has the right to accept this Offer and thereby conclude an Agreement with the Foundation.

3.2. Depositing funds by the Donor to the Foundation 's current account / Foundation account (account) in the payment system by any of the methods provided for in clause 4.3. of this Agreement constitutes the Donor's agreement with the terms of this Agreement (hereinafter referred to as Acceptance of the Offer) and at the same time confirms the voluntary and gratuitous nature of the donation.

3.3. The date of Acceptance of the Offer and, accordingly, the date of conclusion of the Agreement is the date of crediting the amount of money donated by the Donor to the Foundation's current account / account (account) of the Foundation in the payment system.

3.4. The terms of the Agreement with the Donor who accepted the Offer are determined by the Offer in the wording valid on the day the Donor makes a donation to the Fund's account or on the day the Donor issues a payment order, performs other actions, including using mobile communications to the appropriate number, on the basis of which the bank, another organization then transfers the donated funds to the Foundation's current account.

3.5. The place of conclusion of the Agreement is: the city of Nis, Serbia.

3.6. An agreement concluded in the manner provided in this Offer is considered to be concluded in writing.

4. The procedure for making a donation and spending it

4.1. The Donor independently determines the amount of the donation and transfers it to the Foundation under the terms of the Agreement using one of the payment methods specified in clause 4.3. of this Offer.

4.2. The Donor does not set a time frame for using the donation.

4.3. The Donor sends funds to the Foundation using one of the following methods of cash or non-cash payment:

4.3.1. Direct transfer of a donation to the Foundation's current account specified in Article 7 of the Offer, including by payment by a legal entity or individual entrepreneur of a payment order, execution by a citizen of a bank transfer directly through the operating cash desk of the Donor's bank, making a payment in the Donor's personal account on the website of the Donor's bank, debiting funds from the Donor's bank card and others;

4.3.2. Transfer of donations through electronic payment systems and other payment agents, incl. presented on the Foundation's Website, with or without intermediate crediting of the donation amount to the Foundation's account in the payment system;

4.3.3. Transferring donations to special cash collection boxes.

4.4. When making a donation, the Donor, taking into account the provisions of this Agreement, may independently determine the purpose for which the Fund will use the funds transferred by the Donor.

4.5. Regardless of the payment method chosen by the Donor, when transferring funds, the Donor can indicate the project or program to which the donation is transferred.

5. Rights and obligations of the Parties

5.1. The Foundation is obliged to post on the Site reliable and up-to-date information about current projects and programs, their authors (with the consent of the authors).

5.2. Donations sent to the Foundation on the basis of the Agreement are spent by it strictly in accordance with the current legislation of Serbia, the Charter of the Foundation, the provisions of this Agreement, as well as the purpose of the donation established by the Donor, communicated to him when making the payment.

5.3. Donations made by the Donor with an incorrect or indistinguishable (impossible to determine) payment purpose are accepted by the Foundation as donations to achieve the statutory goals of the Foundation.

5.4. The Foundation undertakes to use donations received from the Donor in strict accordance with its statutory activities, existing projects and programs and taking into account the provisions of this Agreement and the requirements of the current legislation of Serbia.

5.5. The Donor expresses his consent and grants the Foundation the right to collect, process and store personal data used by him solely for the execution of this Agreement. The Foundation undertakes not to disclose the Donor's personal and contact information to third parties without his written consent, except in the following cases:

5.5.1. The legal requirement of this information by government agencies having the authority to require such information;

5.5.2. Providing personal data to operators of electronic payment systems that transfer the Donor's funds to the Fund's bank account.

5.6. The donation received from the Donor, due to the closure of the need, is completely spent according to the purpose of the donation specified by the Donor and is not returned to the Donor.

5.7. The Foundation notifies the Donor about current projects and programs by posting information on the Site, electronic mailings, telephone calls and other available methods.

5.8. At the request of the Donor (in the form of an email or regular letter), the Foundation is obliged to provide the Donor with information about the donations made by the Donor.

5.9. The Foundation does not bear any other obligations to the Donor other than the obligations specified in this Agreement.

6. Other conditions

6.1. In the event of disputes and disagreements between the Fund and the Donor under the Agreement, the disputes will, if possible, be resolved through negotiations. If it is impossible to resolve the dispute through negotiations, the dispute must be submitted in accordance with the current legislation of the Serbia for consideration in court at the location of the Fund.

6.2. The Foundation does not bear any other obligations to the Donor other than the obligations specified in this Agreement, as well as those established by the current legislation of Serbia for similar relations.
