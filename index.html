About us: The foundation was founded in 2024 to promote medical innovation in early cancer detection. We want to defeat this disease. Let's make the world a better place together!

The goals of the Foundation are:

01/

Financing the scientific and practical activities of researchers, research centers, medical institutions, scientific projects, laboratories and laboratory research in the field of combating oncological and autoimmune diseases worldwide;

02/

Work on raising awareness and publishing activities related to the issues of oncological and autoimmune diseases and ensuring the efficiency and safety of innovative methods and technologies;

03/

Financing of new forms of diagnosis, treatment, rehabilitation of oncological and autoimmune diseases;

04/

Development of international cooperation for the support and integration of technologies in all countries of the world;

05/

Participation and financing of scientific and practical manifestations at the international, federal and regional level with the aim of constant monitoring and collection of research results, development of innovations in the fight against oncological and autoimmune diseases;

06/

Information on research activities in the field of oncological and autoimmune disease suppression in medicine, as well as the development of academic and professional dialogue in the field of human health and active longevity.

The goals of the Foundation will be achieved

through cooperation with:

International laboratories

Research funding, support for specialists, procurement of research equipment

Innovative projects

In the fiGeld of modern technologies (diagnostic equipment, programs and devices for monitoring body parameters, new findings in diagnostics) - project financing, specialists;

Private international research and medical centers

Financing of research programs, laboratory studies, development of alternative diagnostic, treatment, rehabilitation programs

To private companies

Domestic, foreign and international, working in the field of medicine, technology, marketing and other fields, cooperation with which is necessary to solve the problems of the Fund

If our goals are close to you, join the fight against cancer with us. Your donations, any help to the fund will bring closer the day when this disease will cease to be a death sentence!
